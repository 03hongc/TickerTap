About TickerTap

TickerTap is a lightweight web tool designed to help users quickly analyze stocks using real-time data, sentiment analysis, and basic financial health scoring.

Our goal is to simplify the research process by combining technical metrics and news-based sentiment into one easy-to-read "DD Score" (Due Diligence Score) from 0 to 100.

Whether you're new to investing or just want a faster way to screen tickers, TickerTap helps you make smarter decisions with just a tap.
